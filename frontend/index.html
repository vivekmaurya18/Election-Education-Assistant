The Electoral Roadmap

1. Delimitation & Rolls

Updating boundaries and ensuring every eligible citizen is on the list. The foundation of 'One Vote, One Value'.

2. Notification

The ECI officially sounds the bugle. The Model Code of Conduct kicks in immediately to ensure fairness.

3. Nominations

Candidates file their papers. Scrutiny and withdrawal phases follow to finalize the ballot paper.

4. Campaigning

Political parties share their vision. The campaign ends 48 hours before polling begins (The Silence Period).

5. Polling Day

The big day. Citizens use EVMs and verify their vote with VVPAT. Every vote is a powerful voice.

6. Counting & Results

EVMs are opened in the presence of agents. The results are declared, shaping the future of the nation.
